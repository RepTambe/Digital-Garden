Doing it in public
--

When I first got Instagram in 2012 and twitter in 2013, I felt I could be original. It might have been boyish ignorance, blissful posting with no care or regard. It eventually turned into a "keeping up with the Jones" matter and I felt disconnected from what once brought me freedom to express not only my creative vision, but my honest thoughts.

I started this journey because I no longer want to shelter whats on my mind. This medium will act as my Zettelkasten or "second brain". I'll be free to dump whatever enters my mind.

---
With that in mind, all of these pages are living and open to change/correction. Nothing is set in stone. Just as a human grows and changes throughout their life, so will this page. No longer will I be shackled down with character limits/bland formats/dopamine drainers.
